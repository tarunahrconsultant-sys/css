Clash Royale

A fast-paced, real-time multiplayer tower defense game featuring familiar Clash characters.
Players collect, upgrade, and deploy cards in 3-minute, vertical-screen 1v1 or 2v2 battles.
The goal is to strategize elixir usage to destroy opponent towers while defending your own.
Matches are played in arenas, with victory earning trophies, crowns, and progression to higher leagues.
